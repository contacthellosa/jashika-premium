# Jashika — Creative Rebuild: "Maison of Moments"

## Ground Truth (unchangeable brand core)
- Brand: JASHIKA · PREMIUM GIFTING — a **luxury gifting experience brand** (NOT a tea brand; tea is the medium, the gift is the product)
- Palette: ivory #FAF6F0, cream #F5EFE6, jasmine cream #EFE7D6, ink #1C1C1C, gold #B88E20 / #D4AF37, espresso #241E16
- Typography: Cinzel (brand/labels), Playfair Display (display), Inter light (body)
- Products/prices/Stripe links/testimonials/contact: verbatim from jashika.co.uk
- HARD CONSTRAINT: **NO arch / semi-circle / rounded image frames anywhere.** Sharp editorial rectangles, full-bleed panels, hairline rules only.

## Chosen Direction: "Maison of Moments" (luxury-house editorial)
**Design Movement:** French maison digital editorial — the pacing of Hermès (playful restraint), Cartier (ceremonial depth), Dior (cinematic chapters), The Row (silence as luxury).
**Core Principles:**
1. Cinematic chapters — the page reads like a campaign film: full-bleed imagery alternating with quiet ivory text interludes
2. Sharp editorial geometry — hard-edged full-bleed panels, offset grids, gold hairlines; zero rounded corners
3. The gift is the hero — every image and line centres the act of giving, unboxing, keeping
4. Whispered opulence — few words, large type, generous emptiness
**Layout Paradigm:** alternating full-bleed image chapters and asymmetric 12-col editorial spreads; oversized Roman numerals as chapter markers (I, II, III…); vertical side captions.
**Signature Elements:** gold hairline chapter rules with Roman numerals; vertical letterspaced Cinzel micro-captions; espresso cinematic bands with gold text.
**Interaction:** slow 700ms image scale on hover, 300ms reveals, sticky chapter nav; nothing bouncy.
**Typography System:** Playfair Display up to clamp(3rem,7vw,5.5rem) for chapter titles; Cinzel strictly for labels/nav/CTAs; Inter 300 body.
**Brand Essence:** The maison of gifting rituals — for people who give meaning, not things. Adjectives: ceremonial, intimate, enduring.
**Brand Voice:** short declarative lines, second person, present tense. Ex: "You are not sending a gift. You are marking a moment." / "Kept long after the last cup."
**SEO:** positioned around "luxury gift sets UK", "premium gifting experience", JSON-LD (Organization, Product ×5, FAQPage), semantic landmarks, descriptive alts.

## Style Decisions
- Chapters numbered with Roman numerals I–VI; each opens with an eyebrow + gold hairline
- Full-bleed espresso band uses generated dark pour imagery; ivory chapters use generated editorial stills
- Original product photography retained for the five sets (ground truth product representation)
- Product chapters read as editorial gift portraits: numeral + gift name lead the hierarchy; price and purchase are quiet underlined text actions above a gold hairline, never loud buttons
- The first screen behaves as a campaign opening: one emotional line, one supporting italic line, one gold CTA and one text link — no feature strips or price anchors in the hero itself
- Every major image chapter contains a gifting cue (hands, box, ribbon, card, unboxing); tea pours appear only as ritual atmosphere in interludes

## Boutique Revision (user-directed, supersedes conflicting rules above)
- User feedback: page read too editorial ("like a blog"); buy actions must be evident and easy to click
- Product chapters are now boutique vitrines: image on layered cream panel, price plaque on the image, large price + gold `btn-gold` BUY button in the copy column
- Floating Quick Shop bar (espresso + gold) appears after the hero: all five sets with prices and one-click buy
- Refills carry visible bordered Buy Refill buttons with price
- Hero regains a subtle "Gift sets from £36" anchor line; richness added with ribbon-craft and festive imagery in Manifesto and Occasions
